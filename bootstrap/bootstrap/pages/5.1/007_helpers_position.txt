Fixed top
Position an element at the top of the viewport, from edge to edge. Be sure you understand the ramifications of fixed position in your project; you may need to add additional CSS.
class
"fixed-top"
Fixed bottom
Position an element at the bottom of the viewport, from edge to edge. Be sure you understand the ramifications of fixed position in your project; you may need to add additional CSS.
class
"fixed-bottom"
Sticky top
Position an element at the top of the viewport, from edge to edge, but only after you scroll past it. The
.sticky-top
utility uses CSS’s
position: sticky
, which isn’t fully supported in all browsers.
class
"sticky-top"
Responsive sticky top
Responsive variations also exist for
.sticky-top
utility.
class
"sticky-sm-top"
Stick to the top on viewports sized SM (small) or wider
class
"sticky-md-top"
Stick to the top on viewports sized MD (medium) or wider
class
"sticky-lg-top"
Stick to the top on viewports sized LG (large) or wider
class
"sticky-xl-top"
Stick to the top on viewports sized XL (extra-large) or wider
